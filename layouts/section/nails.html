Nails

Like a lot of people, I started painting my nails to discourage nailbiting. I was quickly drawn in by the amazing techniques of nail techs on Instagram, and what started as a preventative measure soon turned into a full-blown hobby.

I'm not licensed and I mostly work on myself, but I'm pretty decent at doing acrylics and working with gel polish.
